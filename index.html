About

Hi, I am Helen, a creative versatile ROI driven UI/UX- and Product Designer based in Stockholm, Sweden

Last two years i have been working as a full-time employee as UI/UX-Product Designer and Graphic Designer at Vidispine(LINK).

With more than two decades of experience in digital design, graphic art, and visual design, I possess extensive knowledge of design principles, tools, and techniques.

My attention to detail, expertise in colors and typography, and ability to work with various media such as infographics, motion graphics, and video editing enable me to create top-notch B2B content. My guiding principle is to design for the users, not for personal preferences, as I always endeavor to understand their needs and assist them in their daily tasks.
