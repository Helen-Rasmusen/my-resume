About

Creative versatile ROI driven UI/UX- and Product Designer

Hi, I am Helen, based in Sweden, Germany and Portugal. Last two years I have been working as a full-time employee as UI/UX-Designer, Product Designer and Graphic Designer at Vidispine in Kista - “Stockholms Silicon Valley”. Now I’m a gigger, interim consultant, digital nomad and freelancer. You can choose what you need.

With more than two decades of experience in digital design, graphic art, visual design, and you name it, I possess extensive knowledge of design principles, tools, and techniques.

My attention to detail, expertise in colors and typography, and ability to work with various media such as infographics, motion graphics, and video editing enable me to create top-notch B2B content. My guiding principle is to design for the users, not for personal preferences, as I always endeavor to understand their needs and assists in their daily tasks.

    • Award

      SNDs- Society for News Design, Scandinavian Awards

      1991

      Silver Award.
      Category Illustration, print.

      The Society for News Design (SNDs) is a Scandinavian section of an international organization for news media professionals and visual communicators who create print, web, mobile publications and products.

      The members art direct, design, edit, report, illustrate, make photos and video, visualize data and write code. The Best of News Design includes both print and digital categories as part of SND’s annual creative competitions. Judges will recognize journalistic excellence in storytelling, graphics, art direction and social media to identify the visual journalism that pushes the boundaries of design.
